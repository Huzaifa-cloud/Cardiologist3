About Dr. Umer

Dr. Umer Iqbal is a board-certified interventional cardiologist with specialized training in cardiac electrophysiology and heart failure management. He completed his fellowship at Johns Hopkins Hospital and has since led the cardiac catheterization program at the National Heart Institute.

His clinical approach combines the latest evidence-based guidelines with a deeply patient-centered philosophy — ensuring that each patient fully understands their diagnosis, treatment options, and long-term care plan before any decision is made.

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Umer serves on editorial boards for JACC and Circulation, and regularly presents at major international conferences including ACC, ESC, and TCT.
